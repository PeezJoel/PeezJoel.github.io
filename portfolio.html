Forest Dinner Party

Made: 2021

Forest Dinner Party is a solo project I worked on for Runaway Play. They approached me with a game concept and asked me to program it and develop it into a demo. It is a mobile card game about manipulating a board of tiles to combine them into meals.

CyberStar

Made: 2022

CyberStar is an augmented reality escape room game for a mobile device. In it, the player spawns objects into their environment which act as clues to solve puzzles.

Scrapbook Cube

Made: 2022

My Scrapbook Cube is a project that I worked on using cards from Magic: The Gathering. It is a set of cards that I created by cutting and redacting text from common cards. The goal was to create a balanced and funny high-power environment within the game’s rules that can be played in a group.

Neon Premonition

Made: 2022

Neon Premonition is a virtual reality rhythm game. In it, the player must hit boxes floating towards them, similar to the hit game Beat Saber.

Talk Town

Made: 2020

Talk Town is an interactive social game, made to teach social skills to deaf and hearing impaired children. The original creator of the game approached our small team to work on a way to recreate it. I focused mostly on the story and progression of words, and built a dialogue tree based on their studied approach to social responses.

Puddleball

Made: 2020

Puddleball is a two-dimensional video game similar to pool, in which the player controls a pulse which they can use to push a white ball around a map and sink other balls in holes.

Cutbanks: Buy & Sell

Made: 2022

Cutbanks: Buy & Sell is a simple yet chaotic stock trading game for a large group of young people. I created it for a youth event. It contains a story of clues made from news headlines to let players try to predict price changes. It is also a helpful tool to let teenagers understand some basic truths about money and economy.

The Loops

Made: 2019

The Loops is a wood and string puzzle that I built. The puzzle is designed for an office businessman to display on a desk and has an interesting trick to solving.
